TačMi brass band

Brass band from Cerkno-Slovenia has only been established at the end of 2022, so there's not yet much to say about it at the moment, but surely there is a huge potential to expand this description for the future. What separates them from other bass bands from Slovenia is for sure their repertoare, which includes traditional balkan melodies, but also expands to ethnical, oberkrainer and even pop-rock music.
