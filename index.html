About Me

Hello my name is Žan Slabe, and I am from a small town named Cerkno. It is very peacefull town with small amount of people. Currently I am studying at University of Nova Gorica.

Since I was a kid, my biggest passion was allways music. when I was 8 years old I started to learn trumpet at music schooll in Cerkno. During my learning process I attended a lot of competitions and I had a lot of concerts, to learn the culture of a musician.

I finnished my music education at 15 years of age, then I started to form my own music style and character. In the year 2019 I decided to learn a traditional Slovenian instrument called the "Bariton". Bariton is very common in Slovenian folk music and it is used to play recognizable Slovenian rythms.

I play in a lot of bands. I play the trumpet in a local windband, I play the bariton in a brass band, folk ensemble and I have a folk duo with my buddy who plays the Slovenian accordion. Feel free to look around and discover my passion!
