Concert at the Slovenian embassy in Prague

In 2019 I had the opportunity to play at the Slovenian Embassy in Prague. We travelled with the ensemble at the end of June for a weekend. We played at a picnic on the theme of Idrija-Cerkno tourism. The aim of our concert was to present Slovenian folk music to Czech politicians.
