Brass band session at brewery Zajc

In the winter of 2023, we played a very interesting concert with a brass band at the local Zajc brewery. The owner poured top-quality beer and treated us and the guests with top-quality cuisine. With the brass band we created an idyllic atmosphere with good music for a superb evening.
